Numerical simulation tools for computational fluid dynamics (CFD), combustion, and multiphysics — modeling fluid flow, heat transfer, and coupled phenomena with production-grade solvers.

This category covers:

- **CFD solvers** — OpenFOAM and SU2 for fluid flow and aerodynamics
- **Multiphysics** — coupled heat transfer, combustion, and compressible flow
- **Pre-processing** — geometry and mesh generation
- **Post-processing** — extract and analyze simulation results
- **Optimization** — aerodynamic and hydrodynamic design
