Computational fluid dynamics (CFD) tools for modeling fluid flow, heat transfer, and multiphase phenomena with production-grade solvers.

This category covers:

- **CFD solvers** — OpenFOAM, SU2, and other simulation engines
- **Pre-processing** — geometry and mesh generation
- **Post-processing** — visualization and analysis
- **Multiphysics** — coupled heat transfer and flow
- **Optimization** — aerodynamic and hydrodynamic design
