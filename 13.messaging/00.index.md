Desktop messaging clients running on remote compute and streamed to your browser. Each builds on the [Void Desktop](/hub/desktop/void) image and keeps its account and history under `/volume`.

This category covers:

- **Signal** — private, end-to-end encrypted messaging
- **Telegram** — cloud-based messaging

Browse the workflows below. To deploy one, see [How it works](/hub/getting-started/how).
