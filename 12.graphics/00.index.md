Creative graphics applications running on remote compute and streamed to your browser. Each builds on the [Ubuntu Desktop](/hub/desktop/ubuntu) image and opens maximized.

This category covers:

- **GIMP** — raster graphics editing, photo retouching, and compositing
- **Inkscape** — vector graphics for illustrations, icons, and diagrams

Browse the workflows below. To deploy one, see [How it works](/hub/getting-started/how).
